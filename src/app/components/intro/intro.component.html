P Welcome to Poli!

Your AI-powered language learning partner.

Poli is designed to help you practice real-world conversations in your target language. Here's a quick guide on how to get the most out of it:

1 Setup Your Learning Path

First, tell us your native language and the language you want to learn. Then, select your current proficiency level (from A1 to C2). This helps us generate content that's just right for you.

2 Choose How to Practice

You have two great options to start a practice session:

  • Select from Topics: Browse through dozens of real-life categories and topics, from ordering coffee to discussing business. Pick one or more topics to generate a custom conversation scenario.
  • Use Your Own Text: Have a specific text you want to understand better? Paste it in! Poli will break it down, translate it, and create a practice session based on your content.

3 Dive into the Conversation View

This is where the magic happens. You'll see sentences in both your native and target languages.

  • Interactive Vocabulary: Important words are highlighted. Hover over them to see translations and example sentences.
  • Listen: Click the speaker icon to hear the native pronunciation of any sentence.
  • Challenge Mode: Activate this to get instant feedback on your pronunciation and fluency!

That's it! You're ready to start practicing. The best way to learn is by doing, so jump in and have fun. Happy learning!
