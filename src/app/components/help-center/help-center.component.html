Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. If you are already logged in, go to "Settings" > "Account" > "Change Password".

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

We're sorry to see you go! You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time by navigating to "Settings" > "Billing & Subscription". Your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.

My audio recording isn't working. What should I do?

First, please check your device's settings to ensure Poli has permission to access your microphone. If permissions are correct, try closing and restarting the app. If the problem persists, please contact our support team.

What is Adaptive Learning?

Our Adaptive Learning feature (part of the Premium plan) personalizes your lessons based on your performance. It identifies areas where you need more practice and adjusts the difficulty and content of your lessons to help you learn more efficiently.
